Spatial4D-Bench

Abstract: Spatial4D-Bench is a unified benchmark for evaluating spatial intelligence in multimodal large language models (MLLMs). It encompasses dynamic and static 4D spatio-temporal data across five core reasoning categories:

  • Attribute Reasoning
  • Semantic Reasoning
  • Geometric Relationship Reasoning
  • Spatio-temporal Reasoning
  • Common Sense Reasoning
Each category includes video-based question-answering tasks designed to probe models’ understanding of space, time, object properties, and physical plausibility in realistic 4D environments.
